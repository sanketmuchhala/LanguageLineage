About Delphi

Delphi is an Integrated Development Environment for specific dialect of Object Pascal language. It was first released in 1995. Development is led by Embarcadero Technologies.

In the Language Lineage dataset, its toolchain was bootstrapped from Pascal.

Delphi drew on ideas from Pascal and went on to influence C#.
