About Newsqueak

Newsqueak is a programming language. It is a statically typed language. It supports concurrent and imperative programming.

Newsqueak first appeared in 1988 and was designed by Rob Pike. Development is led by Bell Labs.

How Newsqueak is implemented

In the Language Lineage dataset, its compiler is written in C.

Newsqueak in the language family tree

Newsqueak drew on ideas from C and Communicating sequential processes and went on to influence Go.
