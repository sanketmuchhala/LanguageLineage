About Oberon

Oberon is a programming language. It is a statically typed language. It supports imperative and object-oriented programming.

Oberon first appeared in 1987 and was designed by Niklaus Wirth.

How Oberon is implemented

In the Language Lineage dataset, its toolchain was bootstrapped from Modula-2.

Oberon in the language family tree

Oberon drew on ideas from Modula-2 and Pascal and went on to influence Go and Oberon-2.
