About Modula-2

Modula-2 is a programming language. It is a statically typed language. It supports imperative and modular programming.

Modula-2 first appeared in 1978 and was designed by Niklaus Wirth.

How Modula-2 is implemented

In the Language Lineage dataset, its compiler is written in Pascal.

Modula-2 in the language family tree

Modula-2 drew on ideas from Pascal and went on to influence Modula-3, Oberon, and Go.
