About COMTRAN

COMTRAN is a Proposed programming language. It is a statically typed language. It supports imperative programming.

COMTRAN first appeared in 1957 and was designed by Bob Bemer.

How COMTRAN is implemented

In the Language Lineage dataset, its compiler is written in Assembly.

COMTRAN in the language family tree

COMTRAN drew on ideas from FLOW-MATIC and went on to influence COBOL.
