About Clojure

Clojure is a dialect of the Lisp programming language. It was first released in 2007. Development is led by Richard Hickey.

In the Language Lineage dataset, its compiler is written in Java.

Clojure drew on ideas from Scheme, Lisp, and Common Lisp.
