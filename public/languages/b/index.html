About B

B is a procedural programming language. It was first released in 1969 and was designed by Ken Thompson. Development is led by Bell Labs.

In the Language Lineage dataset, its toolchain was bootstrapped from BCPL.

B drew on ideas from BCPL and went on to influence C.
