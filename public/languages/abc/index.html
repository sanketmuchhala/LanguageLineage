About ABC

ABC is a node js. It was first released in 1987 and was designed by Lambert Meertens and Steven Pemberton. Development is led by Centrum Wiskunde & Informatica.

In the Language Lineage dataset, its runtime is implemented in C.

ABC drew on ideas from SETL and went on to influence Python.
