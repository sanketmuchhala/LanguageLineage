About Miranda

Miranda is a programming language. It is a statically typed language. It supports functional programming.

Miranda first appeared in 1985 and was designed by David Turner.

How Miranda is implemented

In the Language Lineage dataset, its compiler is written in C.

Miranda in the language family tree

Miranda drew on ideas from Hope and went on to influence Haskell and Clean.
